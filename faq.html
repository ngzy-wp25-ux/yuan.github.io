Frequently Asked Questions

Starting therapy can feel overwhelming. Here are answers to the most common questions we receive.

General Therapy Questions

How do I know if I need counselling?

If you are feeling overwhelmed, stuck, or finding it difficult to cope with daily life, counselling can help. You don't need to be in a crisis to seek support; many people come to therapy for personal growth, self-awareness, or simply to have a safe space to talk.

What happens during the first session?

The first session is an "intake" session. It's a chance for you and your counsellor to get to know each other. We will discuss your background, your current challenges, and your goals for therapy. It's also a great time for you to ask any questions you have about the process.

What should I do if I feel stress?

Listen to some music which can help you to relax yourself!

Booking, Fees & Insurance

How long is a typical session?

A standard individual counselling session lasts for 60 minutes. We also offer extended 120-minute sessions for more intensive work, particularly for couples or families.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the slot to another client who may need it. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Can I claim this under my insurance?

This depends on your specific insurance provider and policy. We are a registered counselling practice and can provide official receipts for you to submit for reimbursement. Please check with your insurance agent regarding mental health coverage.
