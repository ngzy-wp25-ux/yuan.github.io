Key Assessment Areas

We offer precise evaluation when specific diagnostic clarity is needed to develop a detailed therapeutic strategy. Testing involves standardized measures and clinical interviews.

  • Diagnostic Clarity: Assessments for complex mental health issues (e.g., Depression, Anxiety, Trauma).
  • Psychoeducational:Understanding cognitive profiles, learning disabilities, and academic strengths/challenges.
  • Neurodevelopmental:Screenings and evaluations for ADHD and Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD).
  • Full Report: Every evaluation includes a comprehensive written report and a private feedback session.

What to Expect

The evaluation process involves standardized tests, clinical observation, and detailed questionnaires. The final report provides not just a diagnosis, but a clear, actionable roadmap for therapeutic intervention and support, focusing on concrete next steps.
