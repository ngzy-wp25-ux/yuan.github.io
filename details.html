For Adults

Sometimes being an adult can be difficult, from taking care of children to a gruelling 9-to-5 and stressing out over academics. Maybe all you want to do is hide under a blanket, or escape to a paradise island. Perhaps you just want someone to listen, love, and be loved back.

For Teens & Young Adults

Do you have a mental brick wall? Are you in need of emotional support navigating school, identity, or family pressure? Come find a safe, non-judgmental space here at Solace Counselling.

What We Work On

1. Stress & Time Management
Our trained counsellors provide mental aid to help you develop strategies to cope with stress triggers and create efficient methods to manage your time and energy.
2. Relationship & Family Conflict
Relationships are complex. We help you set boundaries, understand each other better, and communicate openly with honesty and empathy.
3. Self-Confidence & Self-Esteem
We lend a hand to those struggling with negligence or inferiority complexes, ensuring you learn to treat yourself with kindness and stop negative comparisons.
4. Emotion Management
Venting out emotions is healthy but not always easy. Our services help you understand your feelings and learn how to manage emotional outbursts constructively.
5. Anti-Bullying Support
Bullying affects adults and youth alike. We teach efficient ways to cope with the detrimental emotional effects of bullying to protect your future wellbeing.
