Meet Our Counsellors

Our dedicated team is here to support your journey towards clarity and emotional resilience. Get to know the professionals ready to walk beside you.

Nickeisha Clarke

Clinical Director-Youth Specialist

Nickeisha Clarke has over 10 years of experience focusing on youth anxiety, academic stress, and family mediation. She believes in creating practical, achievable coping strategies.

Julia Galleguez

Senior Counsellor-Adult & Couples Therapy

Julia Galleguez on relational therapy and addressing complex trauma in adults. Her approach is rooted in compassion and building self-worth, helping clients navigate life transitions and build stronger emotional bonds.

Vanessa Stanfield

Lead Assessor-Testing & Evaluation

Vanessa Stanfield is our Youth Specialist and Clinical Director. She specializes in creating safe, engaging therapeutic environments for minors and teens, focusing on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) to address youth anxiety, academic stress, and family mediation. She is dedicated to helping young clients build emotional literacy and practical coping skills.

Kimberly Carew

Lead Assessor-Testing & Evaluation

Kimberly focuses on obtaining diagnostic clarity for adults navigating anxiety, depression, and trauma. She specializes in clinical interviews and projective assessments, providing tailored reports that accurately identify core issues for effective therapeutic treatment.

Taylor Blair

Lead Assessor-Testing & Evaluation

Taylor specializes in psychoeducational testing, helping students, parents, and schools understand learning difficulties, cognitive profiles, and academic strengths. Her work ensures students receive the appropriate educational support they need to succeed.

Ashley Johnson

Lead Assessor-Testing & Evaluation

Ashley is a senior evaluator focusing on neurodevelopmental assessments, including screening for Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) and complex ADHD presentations across the lifespan. Her goal is to provide accurate evaluations and compassionate feedback.

Steve Lee Kai Cheng

Lead Assessor-Testing & Evaluation

Dr. Lee oversees all psychoeducational and psychological assessments. She provides clarity on diagnoses such as ADHD and learning disabilities, translating complex findings into practical support plans.
